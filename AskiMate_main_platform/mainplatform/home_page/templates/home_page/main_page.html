About Us

AskiMate is more than a chatbot — it's your AI Mate for studying abroad.

Preparing to study in another country can be exciting — and overwhelming. From choosing the right course and understanding university requirements, to navigating visas, accommodation, and culture shock, there's a lot to figure out.

That's where AskiMate comes in.

As your personal AI Mate, we're here to support you with smart, friendly, and reliable answers — anytime you need them. Behind the scenes, every response is shaped not only by powerful AI but also reviewed and guided by real experts in international university admissions.

Whether you're just starting your journey or have already been accepted, AskiMate helps you move forward with clarity and confidence.

One conversation at a time, we're making studying abroad simpler — and smarter.
